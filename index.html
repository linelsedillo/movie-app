3Useful Tips To Better Your
Movie Watching Experience

Go Solo

The first and best way to watch a movie is to watch it solo. Seeking the company of a friend or an acquaintance may be fun-filling and entertaining, but the risk is, your partner’s thoughts about the film may directly affect your judgment on the film. There will also be more room for discussions while watching it together.

Read Minimal Reviews

This point will make no sense if you are to catch a movie on its first day first show. Because, when you are to watch a movie in its first screening, there is very less chance for you to get exposed to the reviews. Staying completely away from the reviews will help greatly.

Avoid Subtitles

Avoid subtitles if you are familiar with the language. You may feel it is one good way to figure out some new words uttered by the characters in the film. But, many fail to realize that it is okay to miss a word rather than missing the complete rhythm of the film.
